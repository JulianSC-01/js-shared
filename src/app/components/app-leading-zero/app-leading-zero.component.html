Leading zero pipe

Overview

The appLeadingZero pipe is used to pad the absolute value of a fixed rounded number with leading zeros. You may specify the following parameters:

desiredLength (Optional - Default: 2)
This parameter specifies the desired length of the number. If the converted number's length is less than the desired length, it will be padded with leading zeros as needed.
